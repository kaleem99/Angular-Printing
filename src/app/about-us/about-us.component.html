We fully support Government Policy on employment equity. Our plan has been submitted to the Department of Labour by De Loitte and Touche (Human Capital Corporation). Our History

1976: Established as Copy Type Electronics, a supplier of general office machines.

1981: Rebranded to COPY TYPE ELECTRONICS, establishing Endeavour Finance for customer financing options.

2002: Founded KAGO FINANCE and KAGO OFFICE AUTOMATION to support black economic empowerment initiatives.

2021: Obtained rights to import Duplo Digital Copy Printers, establishing headquarters at Lanseria/Randburg.
